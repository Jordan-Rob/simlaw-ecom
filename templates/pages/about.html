About agrikol

We’re Providing The Best Solution

Simlaw seeds company Uganda limited is a subsidiary of Kenya seed group of companies. Simlaw Seeds Company was registered in Kenya as a limited liability company in 2002 and its core business is selling and marketing of high quality horticultural seeds in Kenya and eastern Africa.

In the same year, Kenya Seed incorporated Kibo Seed Co. Ltd and Mt Elgon Seed Company Ltd in Tanzania and Uganda respectively as an expansion strategy into the Eastern Africa market. In Uganda, the company changed its name from Kenya Seeds Co Ltd and Mt. Elgon Seed Company Limited to Simlaw Seeds Company Uganda Limited in the fulfillment of the Board resolution to introduce Simlaw Seeds as a regional and international brand of Kenya Seed Company Limited.

Our Mission

To avail top quality certified seed, through research and technology to our customers timely and affordably to increase productivity

Our Vision

To be the preferred supplier of Top quality seed in Uganda and beyond

We’ve 20 Years Agriculture Experience

Infrastructure

The company has a head office building (rented) at plot 78-84 6th street industrial area, where the main store is located. The store in equipped with cold storage equipments to ensure seed longevity. We also have ware houses (rented) in Kapchorwa, Mbale and Masindi. These stores have a capacity to handle about 500 metric tons. The company also owns three pickups (4WD) and a 10 ton truck which helps in the movement of staff and goods respectively across our areas of operation and affecting our activities in the region. We also own three motor cycles which help our field officers to visit crop fields, farmer fields and carry our product development and marketing

Seed Distribution and Marketing

Our seed distribution and marketing is done in a very well organized system. We have branches in Mbale, Kapchorwa, Masindi and Nakivubo. We have Agents in major towns in Uganda, which helps us to distribute, market and sale our products to all corners of the country and the region. Business collaboration with international Agents in Southern Sudan, Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC), Rwanda and Burundi is also growing which strengthens our export market.
